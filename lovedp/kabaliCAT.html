You can’t fix the stray problem without fixing the source
Trap-Neuter-Vaccinate-Return (TNVR)
TNVR is the most effective and permanent way of controlling the stray cat population in your community. You can be part of the solution by working with your local government and PAWS to organize a TNVR in your community. Get started by Contacting Us for a FREE seminar for your community.
Holding capacity refers to food sources like garbage or feeding spots that attract strays. Removing cats doesn’t solve the problem — others will just take their place. TNVR and managing food sources is the only lasting solution.
