Kapon Outreach Program
The Kapon Outreach Program delivers free or low-cost spay/neuter services to communities through the support of LGUs and generous sponsors. These partners help cover costs like vet supplies, transport, and food or lodging for the team. In exchange, PAWS temporarily closes its low-cost clinic to focus fully on the outreach. To bring the program to your area, coordinate with your barangay, propose a venue, and help with basic community outreach.
With the help of LGUs and sponsors, PAWS brings kapon services to areas in need. Communities just need to coordinate with their barangay and assist with planning so PAWS can do the rest.
